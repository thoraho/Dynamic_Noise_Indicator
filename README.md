# The Sound Wave - Dynamic Noise Indicator

## What this device does
This device listens to the sound in your environment and respons in real time. As noise levels rise, the LED strips shifts from green to red and the motor speeds up. In a quiet room the lights stay green and the motor runs slowly. Raise your voice or play music, and you'll see the colors shift to yellow and red as the motor picks up speed.

## How to use it

1. Place the device on a flat surface
2. __Connect the power__: Plug the device into a USB power source.
3. __Flip the switch__ located near the USB input.
4. The device should now be active. Try it out by speaking loudly or blowing into the microphone located on the side of the device.

## How it responds
* __Quiet environment__: The LEDs glow green, motor runs at low speed.
* __Moderate environment__: LEDs shift toward yellow, motor speeds up.
* __Loud noises__: LEDs turn red, motor runs at full speed.

## Tips
* The device responds best to sustained sound rather than sharp, isolated noises.
* The motor will always run at least at a low speed while powered on. This is by design.

## Powering off
Flip the switch and disconnect the USB cable.
